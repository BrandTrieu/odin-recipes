Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan.
It is usually mixed with ingredients such as vegetables, eggs, and some type of protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1 cup of diced chicken or other protein (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables and protein (if using) and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and crack the eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until partially cooked.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix everything together.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the rice and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
