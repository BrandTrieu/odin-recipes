Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic American sandwich made with peanut butter and jelly spread between two slices of bread.
It is a quick and easy meal or snack, often enjoyed by children and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of jelly or jam (grape, strawberry, etc.)
  • Optional: sliced bananas or honey for extra flavor

Steps

  1. Toast both pieces of bread for 1:30 minutes.
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly or jam evenly on the other slice of bread.
  4. If desired, add sliced bananas or drizzle honey on top of the peanut butter.
  5. Press the two slices together to form a sandwich.
  6. Cut the sandwich in half or quarters if desired. Serve immediately.
